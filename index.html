Mode

    Rules:

  • The goal of the game is to capture the enemy king
  • Units can move in any direction as many times as their movement value.
  • Units can only capture by moving in a straight line
  • To capture an enemy, it must be "engaged" by at least as many units as it's armor value.
  • Enemies with 0 armor don't need to be engaged to be captured
  • Your units can engage enemies in a straight, unblocked line, up to the unit's engage range
  • Mountains block movement and engage; you can't engage someone across mountains, even if your engage is greater than 1
  • If a unit is on water, it can't engage anything

    • Key:

    Dark Green: Unit can walk here
    Dark Red: Tile is blocking your path
    Bright Red: Enemy unit can be captured
    Gray: Enemy unit is being engaged
    Teal: Tile can be passed through
    Blue: This unit is being disabled
    Yellow: Unit can enter but not continue through
